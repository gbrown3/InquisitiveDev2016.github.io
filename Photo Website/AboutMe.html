Ali Haider

Portrait Photographer

I was born in the US, but was raised for most of my life in
Canada. Initially, I used to have very little interest in visual arts and
thought that being good at it was something that you were born with.
However, photography has allowed me to better appreciate the work
that goes into creating great art, and has given me an outlet to
show other people's personalities to the best of my abilities.
